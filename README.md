# Cook

Cook is a desktop English-learning assistant that helps you communicate more naturally while answering your questions.

## Features

- **English conversation practice** — Chat with Cook in English about everyday questions and topics.
- **Helpful corrections** — When your English contains grammar mistakes, awkward wording, or unnatural phrasing, Cook briefly explains the issue and suggests a more natural version before answering.
- **No unnecessary feedback** — If your English is already natural and correct, Cook responds directly without interrupting the conversation with redundant language notes.
- **Chinese-to-English guidance** — Write in Chinese to see how the same idea would be expressed naturally in English, followed by an answer to your question.
- **Context-aware conversations** — Cook uses the conversation history to understand follow-up questions and references from earlier messages.
- **English dictionary support** — Ask about a word to receive a focused explanation with details such as pronunciation, part of speech, meaning, and a useful example.
- **Live responses** — Answers appear progressively while Cook is generating them.
- **Readable at any size** — Adjust the text scale from the settings page, preview the result immediately, or return to your device default.
- **Remembered preferences** — Cook saves your text-scale choice and restores the desktop window's previous size, position, and maximized state.
